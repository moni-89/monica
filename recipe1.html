ugali fish

delicious fish to be eaten with ugali

ingredients

  • 4 cups iof water
  • 2 cups of white maize flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 whole fish(tilapia),cleaned
  • 3tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 garlic cloves,minced
  • 1 tsp ginger,grated
  • 2 tomatoes,diced
  • 1-2 chili peppers
  • 1tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 lemon(juice for marinating)
  • 1 cup of water
  • fresh parsley
    •

    instructions

    1. boil water in a sufuria until it is ready
    2. add flour while stirring using a cooking stick
    3. keep stirring and press the mixture against sides of the sufuria
    4. form a round mound,cover and serve after 3 minutes
    5. marianate the fish using lemon juice,salt and pepper
    6. pan-fry the fish for 4minutes
    7. add onions,garlic ginger and stir
    8. add tomatoes and stir
    9. pour water and cover till its ready
    10. sprinkie fresh parsley
    11. serve and lay the table
