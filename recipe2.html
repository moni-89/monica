chicken rice

delicious rice cooked in chicken broth,served with well cooked chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pishori rice
  • whole chicken(cut into pieces)
  • minced garlic
  • onion,finely chopped
  • 1piece of ginger
  • 3cups of chicken broth
  • salt
  • cooking oil

instructions

  1. wash and soak rice for 15 minutes then drain
  2. in a sufuria,heat oil and saute onions,garlic and ginger until fragrant
  3. add the made chicken pieces and cook until a brown colour is achieved
  4. pour in chicken broth and bring to boil
  5. add rice and reduce to a simmer.Cover and cookfor 15-20minutesa
  6. fluff the rice and serve with the chicken,garnished with spring
